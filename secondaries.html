Secondaries

Confidential execution. Institutional-grade access. Secondary transactions done right.

Discreet Liquidity Solutions for Illiquid Assets

MCM Partners specializes in secondary transactions across private fund interests, direct company stakes, and large public blocks. We offer discreet, efficient solutions tailored to institutional sellers and qualified buyers seeking high-quality assets at attractive valuations.

With over two decades of transaction experience, our team works across asset classes and geographies to deliver secondary execution that prioritizes value, confidentiality, and speed.
