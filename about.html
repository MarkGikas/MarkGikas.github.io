Daniel Rose

Group CEO

Prior to joining MCM, Daniel was CEO and Board Director of VTB Capital Hong Kong (VTBC), overseeing an SFC regulated Investment Banking platform focused on natural resources activities across Global Markets, Structured & Corporate Finance, M&A and Asset Management. In addition to his role as CEO, Daniel led the bank's Asian Commodities business which actively traded physical metals, energy, bulks, agri and provided bespoke financing, equity, credit and derivative solutions to clients across Asia-Pac. Daniel has spent 20 years in the commodity markets and investment banking working for Societe Generale, Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley (prior to VTBC) in Sydney, London, Hong Kong and Singapore. Daniel holds a Bachelor of Law (Honors) and Bachelor of Commerce from Bond University in Australia and serves as a non-executive director of ASX listed Pivotal Metals (ASX : PVT).

Adrian Valenzuela

Former Managing Director and Co-Head of Global Equities Distribution, JP Morgan.

Adrian Valenzuela is Co-Founder and CEO of MCM Partners. He relocated to Hong Kong in early 2011 and joined Barclays Capital as a Managing Director and Head of Distribution for Equity Liquid Markets across the Asia Pacific Region. He played a central role in the firm’s expansion, participating in the hiring of circa 200 staff recruited across sales, trading and research. He was a member of the Global Equities Management Committee, Asia Sales Management Committee, and Asian Equities Executive and Management Committees.

He previously worked for JP Morgan from early 2007 and had several promotions during his time, latterly co-heading the global distribution across cash equities and equity derivatives for the equity division. He was part of the Global Equities Executive Committee, Equity Derivatives Global Management Committee and the Global Cash Equity Management Committee. He managed a sales force of over 500 sales professionals across the globe, delivering sales, trading and advisory to global institutional investors and corporations across equity products.

Between 2006 and 2007, Adrian ran the Alpha Strategies Group for Merrill Lynch International, working on advisory projects for various institutional investors across asset classes, and coordinating the strategic marketing campaigns across the equity division.

In 2005, he was a Founding Partner and Head of Marketing and Product Development for Wealth Management Group (WMG), an alternative asset management firm. He worked extensively in new fund development and capital-raising for various funds.

Previously, Adrian worked at Lehman Brothers International as Head of Pan-European Flow Derivatives Sales, including the coverage of global hedge funds for European derivative markets. He also worked at Morgan Stanley New York from 1998 until 2002 as Head of Equity Derivative Sales for Latin America and in the derivatives sales team for US hedge funds. In early 2002, he transferred to London as a senior salesperson for the European Hedge Fund Sales Desk for equity derivatives, also heading the sales teams for Southern Europe and France.

Adrian holds a BA in Economics from the Instituto Tecnologico Autonomo de Mexico, as well as various other certificates and degrees, including graduating from the Stanford University Senior Leadership Program. He began his career in emerging markets, trading fixed income securities for Santander from 1991 through 1994, later moving to ING Barings as part of their equities, equity strategy and equity derivatives teams.

Angie Ma

Wenbin Dong

Partner and CIO of MCM Investment Partners

Before re-joining MCM Partners, Wenbin worked as Managing Director for AVIC Capital International, the international investment platform of AVIC, a leading central SOE of China, in charge of private equity investment. From 2012 to 2014, Wenbin worked as the Sales Director for Sumberg Advisory, a leading independent research house in Mainland China. Sumberg is renowned for its research on macroeconomics, TMT and thematics. He was also in charge of TMT research, serving the largest institutional investors and sovereign funds across the Mainland. Wenbin started his career with AXA Investment Managers (Investment Solutions Department) before moving to Sociétés Générales in Paris, working on fixed income pricing. Wenbin holds a Master of Science degree in Artificial Intelligence from l’Institut National des Sciences Appliquées (INSA) in Toulouse, France.
